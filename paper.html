REGISTRATION FEE STRUCTURE

Delegates may complete and send the registration form along with the following fee (per delegate) through online mode via SBI collect on or before 10th Aug 2024 .

STANDARD REGISTRATION
Participant Category Indian Delegate Foreign Delegate
Delegates from Educational Institutions: Rs. 4,000/- USD 200
Delegates from Other Organizations: Rs. 5,000/- USD 250
Research Scholars: Rs. 2,000/- USD 100

The Registration fee includes a conference kit, two conference lunches, and one dinner.
